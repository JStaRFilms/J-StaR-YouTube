## Step 1: Concept Validation (4 Avatar Questions)

### 1. Avatar: Who is this for?
**Specific person**: A 20-35 year old developer/content creator who's using AI coding tools (Cursor, Claude Code, Antigravity) but frustrated that the AI makes UGLY designs. They know how to code but aren't designers. They want their projects to look professional without hiring a designer.

### 2. Objection: What do they currently believe?
- "AI is bad at design - I just have to live with it"
- "I need to manually specify every color/font/spacing"
- "Skills are complicated to set up"
- "I need expensive tools or subscriptions"

### 3. Stakes: What do they LOSE if they scroll past?
- Keep wasting time on ugly AI-generated UIs
- Keep manually fixing colors and fonts every project
- Miss out on a FREE system that makes AI design like a pro
- Stay stuck at "developer design" level

### 4. Transformation: Dream Outcome
**Before**: AI spits out generic Bootstrap-looking garbage
**After**: AI generates production-ready designs with proper color theory, typography, and UX patterns - like having a design team in your terminal
